Wintersession Web Design

The course website was central to the day-to-day operation of my class. Each class period began with students logging on to the website, downloading the lecture slides for that day as well as any sample code or design resources I had prepared for them. Students were responsible for maintaining their own hosting account and domain name, where they would put all of their homework each week. Assignments were "turned in" by posting the link to their website in the comments section of the syllabus.
